Cryptography is the practice of securing information by converting it into a coded format. Theory of Games is a mathematical framework for analyzing situations where multiple players make decisions that affect each other. In programming, it involves using algorithms to find optimal strategies in competitive scenarios, such as in economics, politics, and computer science.
